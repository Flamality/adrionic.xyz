Notifications

Stay in the loop with all the latest updates from Adrian's digital universe by joining the Onic Club Discord server! Our trusty bot, Pingcord, ensures that you never miss a beat, promptly announcing the release of new YouTube videos and the commencement of live streams as soon as they go live. But that's not all! For those who prefer to receive notifications directly from YouTube, simply subscribe to Adrian's channel and hit the bell icon, then select 'All.' This ensures that you're always in the know, receiving notifications for every new video and live event.

As for TikTok, while the platform does offer live notifications, we understand that it can sometimes be glitchy. That's why we make it a priority to keep our Discord community informed whenever Adrian goes live on TikTok. To enable live notifications on TikTok, follow Adrian's account and click on the bell icon in the upper right corner. Then, navigate to the 'Adrionic's LIVE videos' section and toggle the 'All' option. With this setup, you'll receive notifications every time Adrian goes live, ensuring that you never miss a moment of the action.
