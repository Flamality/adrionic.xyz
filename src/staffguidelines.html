Guidelines

1. Professional Conduct:

Maintain a professional and unbiased demeanor. Avoid personal conflicts and be impartial.

2. Swift Response:

Respond promptly to mentions. Act quickly and efficiently to resolve issues.

3. Consistent Enforcement:

Apply rules consistently and fairly to all users. Avoid playing favorites; treat everyone equally.

4. Communication is Key:

Communicate clearly with users and other moderators. If clarification is needed, ask for it promptly.

5. Confidentiality:

Keep sensitive information confidential. Do not disclose information about moderation actions unless necessary.

6. Discretion in Punishments:

Choose appropriate sanctions based on the severity of the offense. Escalate sanctions gradually, as needed.

7. Team Collaboration:

Collaborate with fellow moderators and administrators. Share insights and discuss challenging situations.

8. Lead by Example:

Uphold the server rules as a role model. Demonstrate the behavior expected from the community.

9. Stay Informed:

Stay updated on server rules and changes. Be aware of Discord's terms of service and guidelines.

10. Public Relations:

Engage with the community professionally. Address concerns publicly when necessary, but avoid public disputes.

11. Use Mod Tools Responsibly:

Exercise caution when using moderation tools. Only use tools for their intended purposes.

12. Training and Development:

Participate in training sessions and stay informed about new features. Continuously improve moderation skills.

13. Self-Assessment:

Reflect on your actions and decisions. Seek feedback and be open to improvement.

14. Accountability:

Take responsibility for your actions. If a mistake is made, acknowledge it and work towards resolution.

15. Community Engagement:

Engage positively with the community. Foster a welcoming environment while upholding rules.

16. Hierarchy Respect:

Acknowledge and adhere to the hierarchy within the moderation team. Listen and follow the instructions of higher-ranking moderators and administrators, especially in crucial situations. Effective teamwork requires respecting the chain of command.

EXTRA NOTES

Note

Remember, as a moderator, your role is to create a positive and inclusive community. Consistent enforcement, clear communication, and professionalism are key aspects of effective moderation.

Interaction

While interacting on the Discord server, it's encouraged to express yourself authentically. However, in situations where a moderator's involvement is necessary, please approach it with professionalism. Everyday interactions on the server can continue without constraints, but it's important to adhere to the server rules, regardless of whether you have moderator powers.

Violations

Violation of any rules while moderation is necessary will result in a moderation report and a chance of termination of moderation. Along with any other punishment that fits the violation.
