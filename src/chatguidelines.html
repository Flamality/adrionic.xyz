What do the guidelines apply to?

These guidelines apply to all chat / comment system in the Adrionic community including Youtube comments / live chat, Onic Club Discord server, and tiktok comments / live chat

Guidelines

1. Be Respectful

Treat others with kindness, respect, and empathy. Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior.

2. Stay on Topic

Keep the conversation relevant to the current context or topic. Avoid derailing discussions with unrelated subjects.

3. Listen Actively

Pay attention to what others are saying and show interest in their viewpoints. Avoid dominating the conversation or interrupting others excessively.

4. Provide Constructive Feedback

Offer feedback in a constructive and respectful manner. Focus on the issue at hand rather than attacking the person.

5. Be Patient

Allow others time to express themselves and respond. Avoid rushing or pressuring others to respond immediately.

6. Respect Privacy

Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information about yourself or others without consent. Respect the privacy of others in the chat.

7. Use Appropriate Language

Choose your words carefully and avoid using offensive, vulgar, or inappropriate language.

8. Be Open-Minded

Be open to different perspectives, opinions, and ideas. Engage in meaningful dialogue and be willing to consider alternative viewpoints.

9. Report Abuse

If you encounter abusive or inappropriate behavior in the chat, report it to the appropriate moderator or authority.

10. Follow Chat Rules

Familiarize yourself with any specific rules or guidelines for the chat platform or community and abide by them.
