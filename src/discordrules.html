Rules

Rule #1 - Personal Info

Do not post highly personal information on this server, such as home addresses, phone numbers, or anything that may allow someone to find your location, contact your personal devices, or somehow access your personal internet accounts.

Rule #2 - Be Respectful

Harassment, personal attacks, bullying, and anything of the sort will not be tolerated. If someone states they're uncomfortable with something you're saying to them and asks you to stop, please respect their wishes. Repeat violations will ultimately result in a permanent ban.

Rule #3 - Controversial Topics

Talking about notoriously controversial topics is not allowed on this server. This includes discussions involving politics, religion, or anything else that could lead to an argument. This server is meant to be a place where we come together and have fun, and celebrate our similarities rather than argue about our differences.

Rule #4 - No NSFW Content

Age-restricted or obscene content is not allowed on this server. This includes text, images, or links featuring mild to graphic depictions of nudity, sex, hard violence, or other visually disturbing content.

Rule #5 - Self Promotion

No spam or self-promotion in any channel other than the dedicated "promote-yourself" channel. This includes DMing fellow members. There is a dedicated channel for this, so keep all self-promotion to that channel. (Server invites are not allowed in ANY channel)

Rule #6 - Moderation Team

If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let a moderator know. We want this server to be a welcoming space for everyone, and we can all help to do that by holding each other accountable. Moderators are here to keep things running smoothly on the server, and have the ability to mute, suspend, or ban if needed. Please treat them with respect.

Rule #7 - Spam

Do not spam any channel with text, emojis, images, links, or anything else. Violation of this rule will result in a warning, and if ignored, a suspension. Subsequent violations will result in a permanent ban.

Rule #8 - Duplicate Accounts

Only one account is allowed on this server per person. Violation of this rule will result in suspension, and ultimately a permanent ban.

Rule #9 - Roles

Requesting access to both Moderator and YouTube Member roles is not allowed. Moderator applications will be clearly announced when they are open, and removed when they are closed. YouTube Member roles can only be accessed directly through Adrionic, and will be given upon confirmation of YouTube Membership status.

Rule #10 - Channels

Make sure to keep conversations for specific topics in the dedicated channels. For example, all explicitly food-related conversations, images, etc. are to be kept in the food chat channel. Bans will not be given for the occasional off-topic post in most channels, but deliberately malicious off-topic posting will ultimately be met with a ban.

Rule #11 - Discord TOS

You must adhere to Discord's Terms Of Service and Community Guidelines. Links for these can be found below:
